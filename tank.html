As a personal project, I decided to design and build my own 3D tank. The tank is approximately 13 inches long and 8 inches wide, and is designed to achieve a maximum speed of 5 m/s and be able to ascend a 4 inch roadside curb.

Project Details

  • All the mechanical components will be procured through 3D prining or laser cutting
  • Electrical components include: 5000mAh Lithium ion battery, Vex bag motors, 30A ESCs, Arduino Uno and Esplora
  • The stiffness of the tank's suspension will be easily modified by using rubber bands of various elasticities




As of January 2016, I have printed all of the mechanical components except for the base. The video on the right shows testing of treads links and powering gear. Under the correct tension, the tread links do not skip any of the teeth on the gear.
