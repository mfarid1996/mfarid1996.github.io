Quadcopter June 2015

Project Description

Over the summer of 2015, I bought a Quadcopter kit comprised of frame parts and an onboard flight controller. After some research, I purchased the appropriate electronic components, including brushless DC motors, electronic speed controllers a lithium-polymer battery, and a wireless transmitter/receiver.

Project Details

  • Assembling the drone was relatively simple -- configuring the electronics was more difficult.
  • I struggled at first with the ESC configuration; this was resolved by re-wiring a few connections and applying heat shrink tubing.
  • Once I established communication between the transmitter and onboard reciever, calibrating the built in PID constants on the flight controller was more difficult than expected.


I obtained a cheap power distribution board, and borrowed a soldering iron from a friend. The ESCs and battery were soldered directly to the power board, while the connections between the motors and ESCs were done with bullet connectors.
